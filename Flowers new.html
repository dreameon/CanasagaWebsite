Jasmine Oolong

Ingredients:

Organic oolong, rose, jasmine and osmanthus.

Directions:

Put 1 to 2 teaspoon of tea into a pot of 500 ml (17 oz) of cold or hot water (up to 90 ℃ or 194 ℉), steep for 3 minutes or more.
